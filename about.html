About me

I’m a game developer with over five years of experience in game development, including educational and VR games. I’ve implemented gameplay mechanics that shape the player’s experience.

I graduated from the M.S. in Games and Playable Media at the University of California, Santa Cruz.

I've had the opportunity to work in great teams of other passionate game developers, and I hope I can continue doing so.

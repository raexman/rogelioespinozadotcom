The Human Body II:
Circulatory & Skeletal Systems

There was a general desire to do more fun, quirky and science based games in the company, and the schools were requesting more biology games at that time. So we came up with two new additions for the "human body" game series. Following previous games in the series, the player has to put together a biological system. In this case the skeletal and circulatory systems. I worked in improving the gameplay code and making the art assets.

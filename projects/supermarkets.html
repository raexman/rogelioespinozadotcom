Supermarket I & II

Even though these two games were made at different times, I'm grouping them together since they have pretty much the same process. Inspired by "Catch the Letters", I was tasked to create a similar game but with a supermarket theme with the goal to promote healthy and nutritional eating. "Good" food items would fall and you would have to catch them, while "Bad" foods were to be ignored. We played with the idea of having a score system, but we were advised against it by the our education experts. I came up with the conveyor belt setting and made a simplified version of the gameplay I coded for "Catch the Letters". The "sequel" to the supermarket was, overall, a skin change of the first one. I did optimize the code, but overall it was mostly the same.

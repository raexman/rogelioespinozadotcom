Brosaurus Wrecks!

After getting greenlighted, we started full production on the publishable version. Being able to work on making an idea a reality with an actual team is great. Since the Brozilla idea now belonged to whole team, not just me, we set out to explore and find the game behind being a giant monster in VR. A lot of our initial expectations were shattered by our playtests and these helped us focus on what parts of the gameplay needed to be tweaked.

As the Creative Director the main challenge was making a game out of a really intuitive VR experience. I worked out the behaviours of all of the enemies and different levels. I worked closely to our Lead Programmer to better define our scope and tweak mechanics in a way that would reduce time production. Brosaurus Wrecks! it's still in production and you can follow our dev diary at our website.

About me

Hi there! My name is Rogelio A. Espinoza. I'm a Game Designer and Web Developer.

Ever since I started doodling and making my own stories, I knew I wanted to keep doing it forever, so when I was older I decided to study graphic design engineering. It was really fun since I got to learn both creative and logical concepts and applied them to my work.

In my early years I was working, almost exclusively, with Flash. Thanks to my experience in it, I worked as a Rich Media Developer and then as a Educational Game Developer. But between those to jobs, I got to learn how to develop websites with HTML5, CSS3 and Javascript. Boy, did I have a good time! I started working as a freelance web developer in no time and haven't looked back since.

I'm currently working as the Development Coordinator for Sistema Educativo Lia, an educational game platform. I'm also doing freelance work as a Web and UI/UX Developer.
